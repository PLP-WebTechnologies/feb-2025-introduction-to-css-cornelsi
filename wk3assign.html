My Favorite Song writer

Cece Winans

Back ground

Cece winans is an american gospel singer who lives in with her family in Brentwood, Tennessee, where she and her husband pastor the Nashville Life Church. She has written many inspiring songs which include;

  • Goodness Of God
  • That is my King
  • King of Glory
  • Worthy of it all
  • Believe for it
  • No greater
All this songs are super amazing and she sings them with a lot of grace
The songs inspire many and I have heard many testimonies about them.
